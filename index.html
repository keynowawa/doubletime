The Craft

CEREMONIAL MATCHA

Sourced exclusively from the shaded hills of Uji, Japan. Only the youngest, most tender leaves from the first harvest are hand-picked, steamed, and stone-ground to preserve vibrant color and pure umami flavor without any astringency.

PREMIUM OAT MILK

We proudly serve Oatside and So Good Unsweetened as our default milk bases. Their exceptionally creamy, rich texture perfectly complements the matcha without masking its delicate, natural flavors.

L-THEANINE

Unlike coffee, matcha contains L-Theanine, an amino acid that slows the absorption of caffeine into your bloodstream. The result? A smooth, sustained energy boost without the jitters or the afternoon crash.

UMAMI RICH

The hallmark of high-grade Uji matcha. A deeply savory, vegetal sweetness with absolutely zero bitterness.

SAVORY SWEET

The contrast of natural matcha sweetness elevated by premium sea salt. Triggers both sweet and savory receptors simultaneously.

THE TEXTURE

We stone-grind our leaves to microscopic levels so that when whisked, it forms a perfectly uniform, velvety suspension that coats your palate.
